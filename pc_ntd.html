Preventive Chemotherapy NTDs

Preventive Chemotherapy NTDs  (PC-NTDs)

Preventive Chemotherapy NTDs (or PC-NTDs); chemotherapy is simply drug treatable NTDs. It can be controlled, treated and managed by mass administration of medicine to group of people with common features living in same community.

Of the 15 priority NTDs, 5, including Onchocerciasis, Lymphatic Filariasis, Trachoma, Schistosomiasis and Soil-Transmitted Helminths are PC-NTDs.

See our PC-NTD Drug Distribution MDA 

Major Updates in PC-NTDs Elimination

  • Completion of endemicity mapping in all 36 states and the FCT
  • Eradication of Guinea worm in Nigeria in 2013.
  • Interruption of transmission of Lymphatic Filariasis in 5 states and the FCT( Sokoto, Kebbi, Cross River, FCT, Nasarawa and Plateau States)
  • Completion over average of 20 years of treatment for onchocerciasis in 36 states plus the FCT
  • Development of National guidelines for onchocerciasis, Lymphatic Filariasis & Trachoma.
  • Elimination of Onchocerciasis in 2 states (Plateau and Nasarawa)
  • Interruption of transmission of Onchocerciasis in 4 states (Kebbi, Kaduna, Zamfara and Katsina state)
  • Weaning of 10.2 million people from Mass Administration of Medicines) in the country.
